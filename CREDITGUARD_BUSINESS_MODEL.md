# CreditGuard Mobile App - Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## Executive Summary

CreditGuard operates on a **freemium subscription model** with multiple revenue streams, targeting the $11.6B credit monitoring market. Our approach combines essential free features to build user trust with premium services that provide exceptional value for credit improvement and financial wellness.

### Primary Revenue Streams (80% of revenue)
